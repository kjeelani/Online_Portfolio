About Me

Welcome to my webpage. My name is Kaif Jeelani and I am a sophomore at Dublin High. If I'm not practicing USACO (Computing Olympiad) problems or learning new algorithms and data structures, I'm probably working on my nonprofit or am trying to compose or learn a new piece on my keyboard. I've been programming for around 3 years and have been teaching programming for 1.5 years, and mainly program in Python, though I do have experience in Javascript, Java, and HTML.


I am pursuing a field of computer science and buisness because combining programming knowledge and the logic that comes with it into the field of entrepreneurship and buisness is very promising path to success, albiet one that is very risky. I also have a passion in teaching others, and my nonprofit, YCW, aims at teaching students how to program for free in order to pique their interest at this promising and amazing field that can improve any field.


Finally, besides teaching and programming, I love composing, listening to, and playing music on my keyboard. It is a nice breather from sitting on a computer screen all day, and it really helps freshen up the mind and soul. People say that music and math have a very strong correlation, and I agree wholeheartedly, as the logical appeal of music is definitely as appealing as programming is!

There are monsters to fight, puzzles to solve, and different boss encounters depending on your choices. Snake Game
